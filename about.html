who we are.

We are registered non for profit private school in Uganda as a CBO organisation. MIRIAM SMITH JUNIOR ACADEMY is founded to honor the strong friendship of Henry and Miriam who met in 2013.

They worked together toward the care and education of Henry’s children and other impoverished children in Uganda.

They worked together, with others, to build a teaching farm for students of agriculture and surrounding villages. Ultimately, Henry’s goal was to build a school for children such as those he adopted from the streets.

Our vision.

The 5 Year MIRIAM SMITH JUNIOR ACADEMY Vision.

Our vision is that between 2021 and 2025 over 1,000 and more children will have received free but quality education in adequate facilities. MIRIAM SMITH JUNIOR ACADEMY has a responsibility to its donors to make sure that all donations and grants result in changing the lives of our students and their learning environment.
