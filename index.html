About Us

Education profoundly affects children, their community, Uganda, Africa, and the world at large. Educated children are an investment in the growth and economy of Uganda.

Educated children are more likely to find successful employment to secure an empowered future for them and their family. Breaking the cycle of ignorance will help to lower the likelihood of poverty.

Educated children become invested in a brighter future just by virtue of being immersed in the academic, social, structured, nourishing, safe environment of school. Children in school are protected from the violence of the streets, where they have to beg for survival.

Educated children are better informed about how to care for their health and well-being and, thus, reduce the chances of forced child marriage, early pregnancy, and the contraction of HIV/AIDS. Educated children evince higher levels of tolerance of differing opinions and cultures, support the formation of positive alliances, and increase the likelihood of positive action since critical discernment levels will rise. The education of children will substantially increase their self-confidence and self-esteem. Educated children will grow into educated adults.
